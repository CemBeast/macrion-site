Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Macrion (“the app”) is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. In short: the app does not collect personal data, has no user accounts, and shows no ads or trackers.

Information stored on your device

All of the information you enter — your age, height, weight, sex, activity level, goals, food entries, and daily logs — is stored only on your device. It is never uploaded to us or to any third-party server, and we have no ability to access it. Deleting the app removes this data from your device.

Nutrition lookups

When you search for a food or scan a barcode, the app sends the search term or the barcode number to two public nutrition databases in order to retrieve nutrition facts:

  • USDA FoodData Central — for food name searches (fdc.nal.usda.gov).
  • Open Food Facts — for barcode lookups (openfoodfacts.org).

Only the search text or barcode you look up is sent. No personal identifiers, account information, or your saved logs are included in these requests.

Camera

The app requests camera access only so you can scan product barcodes. The camera feed is used on-device to read a barcode and is not photographed, stored, or transmitted.

Analytics, tracking, and ads

Macrion contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no cross-app or cross-site tracking. We do not build a profile of you.

Children

Macrion is a general-purpose wellness tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Not medical advice

The macro and calorie targets Macrion calculates are estimates for general wellness and are not medical or nutritional advice. Consult a qualified healthcare professional before making dietary changes.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “last updated” date.
